About us

Portable Cabin Manufacturers

AM Portable Cabin Solutions is a fast growing company dealing in multi-purpose portable buildings,ACs , HVAC systems etc based in Bangalore, India.We deliver high-quality products and services at excellent prices to all destinations worldwide. Years of experience, expertise and skill along with modern facilities, equipment and technology has given shape to extraordinary products and services to meet standard specifications.


Our group of professionals are expert in their particular field of work that has helped us to become a significant player in the market. The remarkable capability of our professionals has further helped us to make a mark in the industry. Due to the dedication of our designers, contractors, quality controllers, sales professionals and others, we are handling high requirements in our range of prefabricated structures,cabins,HVAC systems etc.

OUR SPECIALS

Our Bunk House are manufactured under conditions where we meet the customer requirements even at remote & isolated sites, which can be used as Living Modules or Office Units, Recreation Rooms Kitchen Units, Mess Units, Toilets, and Shower Units.

Our offered Bunk Houses are designed & developed having highest structural stability, weather-proofness, leak-proofness, & faultless operation under extreme climatic conditions.

We are engaged in fabricating a wide range of Portable Cabins that are offered in various forms. Available in variety of colors, sizes, patterns & also as per client's specification of construct, our panels enable us to offer variety in office cabin design & construction.

We cater to banks, NGOs, corporate houses, base camps, security agencies, project execution companies etc., with our range of porta cabins / office cabins.

We install portable kitchen structures manufactured at our facility. These kitchen are fire resistant and have good thermal & insulation properties due to usage of quality material. We have designed weather proof porta room that are ideal for kitchen and can be delivered in one piece at project site, farm or factory locations.

The kitchen assembly includes profiles, doors, partition, ceilings, Modular Kitchen fittings, Kitchen Cabinets, Wall Paneling etc., Which can be easily installed in 4-5 hours..
